How To Play

Game Setup

  • Each player picks 3 cards for their deck
  • Place them face-up in front of you
  • Your first card is your active card — it attacks and gets targeted
  • The other two are benched

How to Win

Knock out all of your opponent's cards!

Turn Sequence

  • Gain 2 energy at the start of your turn
  • Use energy to activate an ability, swap your active card, or skip
  • Max 10 energy at any time

Ability Types

  • Attack — deals damage to opponent
  • Heal — restores your card's HP (can exceed max!)
  • Blocking Shield — blocks damage from the next attack
  • Skipping Shield — skips the opponent's next turn (once per card)

Energy Costs by Color

Purple — 1 Blue — 2 Green — 3 Lt. Orange — 4 Orange — 5 Red — 8

Extra Rules

  • When HP = 0, the card is dead — replace it with the next card to the right
  • Skipping Shields can only be used once per card
  • While your turn is skipped, you still gain 2 energy
  • HP can exceed the card's base HP through healing
